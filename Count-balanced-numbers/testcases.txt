Input: nums = [11, 22, 12321, 4554]
Output: 3
Explanation:
11 → (odd: 1, even: 1) ✅  
22 → (odd: 2, even: 2) ✅  
12321 → (odd: 1 + 3 + 1 = 5, even: 2 + 2 = 4) ❌  
4554 → (odd: 4 + 5 = 9, even: 5 + 4 = 9) ✅  
=> 3 balanced numbers
